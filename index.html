OUR VISION

A space that lets you slow down…

Welcome to Timber Ridge Ranch, one of the Okanagan's most unique mountain stays.

Set on 30 private acres just outside Vernon and minutes from SilverStar Mountain Resort, this is the kind of place people find once and keep coming back to. It's quiet, open, and feels like your own space the second you get here.

At 4,600 sqft with 11 beds, you can pack this place out and still have room to breathe.

Known for its incredible valley view, private setting, and direct access to miles of Crown land, Timber Ridge Ranch is built for both adventure and slowing down. Winters are for skiing and snowboarding. Summers are for riding, biking, and being outside from morning to night.

As the Okanagan's only horse-friendly lodging, the property is equipped with pastures and shelter to welcome both you and your animals comfortably, making it a go-to for riders, families, and groups looking for something a little different.

Our space is set up to host corporate retreats, wellness retreats, family reunions, weddings, and more. It works just as well for a big group as it does for a quiet weekend away.

Oh…did I mention we have ducks?

Whether it's your first stay or your fifth, we're excited to have you here.

The Timber Ridge Family

ABOUT THE PROPERTY

Your Private Retreat in the Okanagan

Our ranch is set in the heart of the North Okanagan on Silver Star Mountain. A true destination that works for both homebodies and adventurers alike. We're just 15 minutes from downtown Vernon, its lakes, parks, wineries and golf courses, but once you're here, it feels like you're properly away. With SilverStar Mountain Resort and the BX Falls close by, the location makes it easy to do as much or as little as you want.

With 6.5 cozy bedrooms and space for up to 18 guests, the home is set up for families and groups looking to unwind in an upscale, backwood-style setting. Each room is designed for comfort, giving you a quiet place to land after a full day.

Some of the extras include a fully stocked coffee bar, wood-burning fireplace, outdoor firepit, BBQ, arcade game, ping pong table, grassed areas for outdoor games, plenty of parking, hot tub, and a full game cupboard.

During your stay, you'll meet our resident animals. Our ducks will always take frozen peas if you offer. We always throw in a complimentary dozen eggs. Our horses, Bluejeans and Spartan, are just as friendly. Let us know if you'd like to feed them! We always have treats and brushes on hand for guests who want the full Timber Ridge experience.

Whether you're here for a ski trip, a family reunion, a wedding, or a quiet getaway, Timber Ridge Ranch offers a true wilderness setting without giving up comfort or functionality. It's the kind of place where memories just happen.

As a family-owned and operated retreat, we take a lot of pride in creating a space that feels welcoming and easy to settle into.

MEET YOUR HOST

Hi, I'm Morgan

Your host at Timber Ridge Ranch

I'm a mom, a host, and part of the Timber Ridge family.

I didn't grow up planning any of this. I've mostly just followed what felt right and figured things out as I went.

Timber Ridge looks like a big operation, but it's really just me and my dad. He handles a lot behind the scenes, I handle the guest side, and between the two of us it stays pretty hands-on.

Most days I'm doing a bit of everything. Running the ranch, goofing off with my kiddo, training Muay Thai, or thinking about what needs to be better next. And sometimes just shutting it all off for a minute.

I'm big on things feeling real. Not staged, not overdone. Just good spaces, good people, and something that actually feels like it's being lived in.

I'm usually outside doing something, fixing something, or changing something. And when I'm not, I'm probably catching frogs.

WEDDINGS AT TIMBER RIDGE RANCH

An unforgettable setting for your special day

We'll be your backdrop.

Timber Ridge Ranch is meant to be shaped around you. The space, the views, the way the day unfolds, it's yours to decide.

Our first wedding here, A and N, set the tone for everything. They built their entire day from the ground up, completely their own, and it was one of those weddings that stuck with me. A lot of the photos you'll see are from their day, and they really show what this place can become.

Since then, we've put together a list of local vendors and services who have been used on the property time and time again since A and N's wedding. You're free to do it your way, but you're not doing it alone. We'll be there every step of the way.

There's something about celebrating out here... The space, the quiet, the way everything slows down a bit. It gives you room to be fully in it, with your people, without feeling rushed or pulled in a hundred directions. And that's what people remember.

We've poured a lot into this place. It's not just a venue to us. It's somewhere people come together to slow down. A place where love lives.

We're deeply passionate about what our ranch becomes for our guests and we honoured to be a part of your special day!

So, if this feels like everything you've been picturing, we're really glad you found us.
